Remote Work Policy
==================

1. Hybrid Work Schedule
Employees are eligible to work remotely up to three days per week, subject to manager approval. Tuesday and Thursday are designated as Mandatory Core Days, during which all team members are required to attend the office in person for alignment and sync meetings.

2. Home Office Ergonomics
To ensure employee well-being, the company offers a one-time stipend of $500 to purchase ergonomic office chairs, desks, or monitors. Employees must submit receipts to the Finance department via the expense portal within 30 days of purchase for reimbursement.

3. Remote Internet Stipend
The company provides a monthly internet and mobile connectivity stipend of $50, added automatically to the monthly salary payment. Employees must verify that their remote connection has a minimum bandwidth of 50 Mbps down and 10 Mbps up to ensure smooth work calls.
