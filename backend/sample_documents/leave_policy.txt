Leave Policy Guidelines
=======================

1. Annual Paid Time Off (PTO)
Each full-time employee is entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of 2.08 days. Unused leave can carry over to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days, which must be spent before March 31.

2. Sick Leave
Employees are eligible for up to 10 days of fully paid sick leave per year. For sickness lasting longer than 3 consecutive calendar days, a valid medical certificate from a licensed practitioner must be submitted to the HR department via the HR portal.

3. Parental and Family Care Leave
The company provides 16 weeks of fully paid maternity leave and 4 weeks of fully paid paternity/partner leave. Additionally, employees may take up to 5 days of unpaid family emergency leave annually to care for immediate family members in crisis.
