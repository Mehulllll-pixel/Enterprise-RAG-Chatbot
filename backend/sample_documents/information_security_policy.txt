Information Security Standards
==============================

1. Data Encryption
All corporate and customer data must be encrypted both in transit (using TLS 1.3 or higher) and at rest (using AES-256 encryption standards). Data backups must be stored in secure, geographically isolated cloud servers and encrypted with rotated keys.

2. Password Standards
All employee account passwords must satisfy the following complexity constraints:
- Minimum length of 14 characters.
- Must contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (e.g. !, @, #, $).
- Passwords must be rotated every 90 days. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all console logins.

3. Clean Desk Policy
Employees must lock their screens (using Win+L or Cmd+Ctrl+Q) whenever leaving their desks, even for short breaks. No confidential paper documents should be left unattended on desks overnight; all printouts must be shredded after use.
